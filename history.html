A Brief Timeline of Taylor Swift's Career

December 13, 1989

Taylor Swift is born in Pennsylvania. She later moves to Nashville at age 14.

October 24, 2006

She releases her debut album called Taylor Swift, a collection of country music. The Single "Time McGraw" becomes a Top 20.

November 11, 2008

Taylor releases Fearless as just an 18-year-old.

September 13, 2009

Taylor wins Best Female Video at the MTV Video Music Awards

January 31, 2010

Taylor earns Album of the Year (Fearless) and is the youngest artist to ever win this at the Grammys.

October 25, 2010

Speak Now is released.

August 21, 2012

"We are Never Ever Getting Back Together" becomes the single with the most digital sales for a female artist.

August 23, 2019

Taylor Swift releases the Lover album

She's releasing her Midnights album on this date!!
